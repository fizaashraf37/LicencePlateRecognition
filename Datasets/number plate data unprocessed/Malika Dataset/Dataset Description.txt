License Plate Detection and Recognition Dataset


Description:
Thius datasset is collected from different sources like google, and videos from multiple areas of diffrent cities.
The dataset covers maximum scenarios like illumination(Day or Night), weather conditions, occlusion etc. There are total
10000 images collected from different cities (Lahore, Islamabad, Karachi, Multan, KPK) and 500 videos of equal 
duration.   

Videos: 500
Images: 10,000
Categories: 37 (26 for each Alphabet, 10 for each digit, and one for license plate)
